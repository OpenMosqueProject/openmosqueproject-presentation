Open Mosque Project

Every mosque should have a website!

The idea behind Open Mosque Project is that every mosque or masjid should be able to simply inform local residents and passers by of information about themselves and prayer times.
Setting up a website may seem complicated for many who administrate a mosque. We aim to simplify this by offering a very simple sign up process.
Our templates are also offered free in order for those with some extra support to be able to customise them further.
Our project is open source. This means that anyone can take our code, improve it and use it for free.
